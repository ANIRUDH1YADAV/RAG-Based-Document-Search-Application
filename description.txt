**Retriever Tool Instruction: Resume Information**

**Purpose:** This retriever tool is designed to assist in extracting information from uploaded resumes for the purpose of answering questions related to job applications.

**Usage:**

1. **Upload a Resume:** Select the resume file you wish to query.
2. **Enter a Question:** Type a specific question about the uploaded resume, such as "What are the candidate's work experience highlights?" or "What skills are mentioned in the resume?"
3. **Retrieve Information:** Click the "Retrieve" button to extract relevant information from the resume and provide a response to your question.
4. **Review Response:** Review the response provided by the tool to ensure it accurately answers your question.

**Important Note:** This retriever tool is only intended for answering questions about the uploaded content. It is not a substitute for human judgment or evaluation. Use the information provided by the tool to inform your decisions, but always consider multiple sources and perspectives when making important decisions.

**Disclaimer:** The accuracy and completeness of the information provided by this tool are not guaranteed. If you are unsure about the information provided, please verify it through other means.
